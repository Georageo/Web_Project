FAQ

Who is eligible for the employee car leasing program?

Employees of partner companies that have enrolled in our corporate leasing program are eligible. If you're unsure whether your employer is part of our network, feel free to contact us or check with your HR department.

What are the benefits of leasing a car through my employer?

Leasing through your employer often comes with exclusive benefits like lower monthly payments, no upfront deposit, inclusive maintenance packages, and potential tax advantages depending on your region.

What documents do I need to lease a car as an employee?

You'll typically need a valid driver’s license, proof of employment, and recent payslips. Additional documentation may be required based on your credit profile or company agreement. These documents must be sent to your firm's HR.
